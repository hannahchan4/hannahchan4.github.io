Introduction

Mindless Matter

Mindless Matter is a small jewelry business located in San Luis Obispo that is committed to bringing clarity to the jewelry industry. We engage in ethical and sustainable business practices, and create high-quality pieces that bring joy to those who wear them. We are proud to be a business that continues to create affordable pieces that can be enjoyed by everyone! We believe that luxury doesn't have to be exclusive.

Target audience

The target audience of Mindless Matter is women ages 18-50 who regularly wear jewelry. These women are either looking to start incorporating jewelry pieces into their style, or are interested in adding to their collections.

The target audience's primary goal is to find pieces to add to their jewelry collection or find pieces that compliment their outfits at an affordable cost.
